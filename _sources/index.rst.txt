synth-dynamics
==============

**Time-Resolved Ensemble Generator using Coarse-Grained ENM Langevin Dynamics**

`synth-dynamics` is a fast, simplified molecular dynamics engine designed to generate meaningful conformational trajectories without the overhead of all-atom simulations. It uses the Anisotropic Network Model (ANM) and Langevin dynamics to capture global protein motions.
